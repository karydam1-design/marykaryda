Mary Karyda

Trained as artist, design researcher, and interaction designer. Interested in data matters.

I am the Lead Researcher of the Ecology and Action Lab at the Moholy-Nagy University of Art and Design. In my research, I am interested in how different data forms and formats can act as prompts for dialogue, reflection, and meaning-making in socio-ecological contexts. My work takes shape through participatory design workshops and interactive prototypes that invite diverse stakeholders to collectively surface and negotiate knowledge.
